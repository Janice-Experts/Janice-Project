
- Backend: validation & suggestions.
- Frontend: results & dashboard display.
- Dashboard: charts of errors and auto-fix stats.

---

## Target Users
- Small clinics & polyclinics.
- Outpatient centers & labs.
- Billing companies handling multiple clients.

---

## Constraints & Assumptions
- MVP supports SA ICD/CPT codes only.
- Minimal patient data stored.
- Users responsible for CSV formatting compliance.
- Runs on local machine or internal server.

---

## Success Metrics
- ≥70% reduction in rejected claims due to coding errors.
- Reduction in staff time spent correcting claims.
- Positive adoption feedback from pilot clinics.
